    Study Tips for ADHD

    Study Tips:

    1. Break down tasks into smaller, manageable steps.

    2. Use a timer (like Pomodoro) to stay focused in intervals.

    3. Take regular breaks to refresh your mind.

    4. Minimize distractions by creating a quiet and organized study environment.

    5. Use tools like reminders and checklists to stay on track.
